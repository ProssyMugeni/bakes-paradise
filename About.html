All About Bakes Paradise

Our Story!

Bakes Paradise was born out of passion to provide the community with quality, tasty and all natural pastries. We believe dessert do not signal the end of the party, but rather another memorable moment to cherish in all throughout the day.

Mugeni Prossy, owner and pastry chef of Bakes paradise, has over 25yrs of experince in proffessinal pastries- from institutional hospitality to five star hotels. She started her bakery education in Uganda, East Africa, graduated with honors - City & Guilds of London Institute.
